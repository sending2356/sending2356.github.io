How It Works

A seamless three-step process.

1. Scan

A customer scans a QR code placed at a specific location using their phone's camera.

2. Tap

They are taken to a simple webpage where they tap a single button to confirm their request for help.

3. Notify

A notification instantly appears on the worker dashboard with the customer's location.
